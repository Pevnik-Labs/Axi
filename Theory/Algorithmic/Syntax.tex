\begin{frame}{Syntax}

\begin{frame}{Judgements}

Kind inference judgement: $\inferkind{\Gamma}{A}[r]{\Gamma'}$ \\
Type conversion judgements: $\infertypeconv{\Gamma}{A}{B}{\Gamma'}$, $\infertypeconvwhnf{\Gamma}{A}{B}{\Gamma'}$ \\

Type checking judgement: $\checktype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\Gamma'}$ \\
Type inference judgement: $\infertype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\Gamma'}$ \\

Term conversion judgements: $\checktermconv{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$, $\infertermconv{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$, $\checktermconvwhnf{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$, $\infertermconvwhnf{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$ \\

Proposition checking judgement: $\checkprop{\Gamma}{P}{\Gamma'}$ \\
Proposition conversion judgements: $\checkpropconv{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$, $\checkpropconvwhnf{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$, $\inferpropconvwhnf{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$

\end{frame}
