\section{Syntax}

\begin{frame}{Syntax}

The syntax in the algorithmic system is the same, but we will have far fewer annotations in terms

\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Judgements}

Kind inference judgement: $\inferkind{\Gamma}{A}[r]{\Gamma'}$ \\
Type conversion judgements: $\infertypeconv{\Gamma}{A}{B}[r]{\Gamma'}$ (all types), $\infertypeconvwhnf{\Gamma}{A}{B}[r]{\Gamma'}$ (types in whnf) \\

Type checking judgement: $\checktype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\Gamma'}$ \\
Type inference judgement: $\infertype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\Gamma'}$ \\

Term conversion judgements: $\checktermconv{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$ (all terms), $\infertermconv{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$ (all terms), $\checktermconvwhnf{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$ (whnfs), $\infertermconvwhnf{\Gamma}{e_1}{e_2}{A}{\Gamma'}$ (whnfs) \\

Proposition checking judgement: $\checkprop{\Gamma}{P}{\Gamma'}$ \\
Proposition conversion judgements: $\checkpropconv{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$ (all propositions), $\checkpropconvwhnf{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$ (whnfs), $\inferpropconvwhnf{\Gamma}{P}{Q}{\Gamma'}$ (whnfs)

\end{frame}
