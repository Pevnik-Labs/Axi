\begin{frame}{Notation}

$\checktype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\extend{\Gamma'}[\totalqty{r}{A}]{x}{A}}$ is a shorthand for $\checktype{\Gamma}{e}{A}{\extend{\Gamma'}[r]{x}{A}}$ with the condition $\subusage{\totalqty{\One}{A}}{r}$.
